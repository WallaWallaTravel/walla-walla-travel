# Driver Quick Start Guide

Welcome! This guide walks you through everything you need for your shift.

---

## 1. Logging In

1. Open **wallawalla.travel/login** on your phone
2. Tap **"Driver Portal"** (the copper/brown button)
3. Enter your email and password
4. You'll land on your **Dashboard** showing today's tours

> If you have trouble logging in, contact dispatch at (509) 200-8000.

---

## 2. Clock In (Start of Shift)

1. From your Dashboard, tap the **clock icon** in the bottom nav bar (or go to **wallawalla.travel/time-clock/clock-in**)
2. Your phone will ask for location permission — **tap Allow** (this records where you started your shift)
3. Select your **vehicle** from the list (your assigned Sprinter)
4. Tap **"Clock In Now"**
5. You'll see a confirmation and be taken to the Time Clock Dashboard

**What you'll see after clocking in:**
- Your name and vehicle assignment
- Today's driving hours (starts at 0.0)
- HOS compliance status (green = good)
- Quick action buttons for inspections

---

## 3. Pre-Trip Vehicle Inspection

1. From the Time Clock Dashboard, tap **"Pre-Trip Inspection"**
2. Walk around the vehicle and check each item on the checklist:
   - Tires, brakes, lights, fluid levels, mirrors, etc.
3. Enter the **starting mileage** from the odometer
4. Check off each item as pass/fail
5. Note any defects found
6. Tap **"Submit Inspection"**

> A pre-trip inspection is required by DOT regulations before every shift.

---

## 4. View Today's Itinerary

1. Tap **"Home"** in the bottom nav (or go to **wallawalla.travel/driver-portal/dashboard**)
2. Your assigned tours appear as cards sorted by pickup time
3. Each card shows:
   - **Pickup time** and location
   - **Customer name** and party size
   - **Number of stops** (wineries)
4. Tap a tour card to see **full details**:
   - Complete list of winery stops with arrival/departure times
   - Customer phone number (tap to call)
   - Special requests or dietary notes
   - Driver notes from dispatch
5. Tap **"Open in Google Maps"** for turn-by-turn navigation to all stops

---

## 5. During the Tour

- Use the **tour detail page** to see each stop's address and timing
- Tap any winery address to open it in Google Maps
- If you need to call the customer, tap their phone number
- Driver notes from dispatch appear in an amber banner at the top

---

## 6. Complete Tour & Collect Tips

1. After dropping off the last guests, go to the tour detail page
2. Tap the green **"Complete Tour & Collect Tips"** button
3. **Step 1 — Lunch Expense**: Enter your lunch amount (if applicable) or tap "Skip"
4. **Step 2 — Receipt Photo**: Take a photo of your receipt (or skip)
5. **Step 3 — Tip QR Code**: A QR code appears that guests can scan to leave a tip
   - You can also share the tip link via text message
   - The tip code is displayed for manual entry

---

## 7. Post-Trip Inspection

1. From the Time Clock Dashboard (wallawalla.travel/time-clock/dashboard), tap **"Post-Trip Inspection"**
2. Enter the **ending mileage**
3. Check each vehicle item
4. Report any new damage or issues
5. Submit the inspection

---

## 8. Clock Out (End of Shift)

1. Go to the Time Clock Dashboard (tap clock icon in bottom nav)
2. Tap **"Clock Out"**
3. Optionally add notes about your shift
4. The system calculates your total hours automatically
5. You'll see a summary of your shift

---

## 9. View Your Schedule

- Tap **"Schedule"** in the bottom nav or go to **wallawalla.travel/driver-portal/schedule**
- See your tours for the entire month
- Color-coded by status: green (confirmed), yellow (pending), blue (completed)

---

## 10. View Documents

- Go to **wallawalla.travel/driver-portal/documents**
- See your compliance documents (MVR, Background Check, Drug Test, Medical Card)
- Upload new documents when they expire

On every driver page, you'll see a fixed bar at the bottom of the screen with 4 icons:

| Icon | Label | Goes To |
|------|-------|---------|

| Calendar | Schedule | Monthly schedule view |

| Clipboard | Inspect | Pre-trip inspection |
