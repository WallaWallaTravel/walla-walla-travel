### **Failure Mode 2: Expertise Doesn't Match Promise**

**Symptoms:**

- Winemakers don't respect you
- Clients feel misled

**Prevention:**
- Only launch when genuinely ready
- Study extensively

### **Failure Mode 3: Operations Break Down**

**Symptoms:**
- Late to pickups
- Booking system confusing

- Test everything multiple times
- Build in buffer time
- Automate where possible
- Have backup plans

- Bachelorette parties expecting party bus
- Budget customers complaining about price
- Groups wanting 7 wineries in 4 hours

**Prevention:**
- Clear positioning on website
- Premium pricing filters appropriately
- Consultation call before confirming
- Okay to say "not a good fit"
