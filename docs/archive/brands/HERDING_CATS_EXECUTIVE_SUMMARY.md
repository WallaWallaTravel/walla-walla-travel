- "HERDING CATS" in elegant serif
- "Wine Tours" in smaller refined font
- Multiple variations (full, icon-only, horizontal)

---

### **Voice & Tone:**

**5 Core Attributes:**

1. **VERBOSE & ARTICULATE**
   - Use more words than necessary (it's charming)
   - "We shall collect your party from the designated coordinates..."

2. **DRY WIT**
   - British comedy, not slapstick
   - Humor delivered with straight face

3. **WINE-EDUCATED**
   - Discuss malolactic fermentation casually
   - Use terroir, varietal, tannin structure naturally

4. **FELINE METAPHORS (SUBTLE)**
   - "Much like our feline namesakes..."
   - Never forced cat puns

5. **SELF-AWARE**
   - Acknowledge task is technically impossible
   - "We persist regardless"
